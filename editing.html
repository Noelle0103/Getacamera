How does one edit photos?

Photograph editing is a nuanced subject with about as much breadth as it has depth. If taking a photograph is drawing a sketch of a painting, then editing the photo is renering that painting in acrylics. It's the step that brings the idea you had in your head when you shot, or perhaps a new idea entirely!

When I refer to editing, I do not refer to using Photoshop to manipulate the image - though that is perfectly valid. I refer to editing as adjusting the color and light values of an image in a non-destructive manner.
