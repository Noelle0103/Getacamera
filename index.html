You Should Get a Camera!

These are all photographs taken with a digital camera - a DSLR (digital single-lens reflex) to be specific. Taking pictures with a camera, as opposed to a smartphone, gives the user a lot more control and options in their photography. A photographer gets control of the lens they use, exposure settings like aperture, ISO (a way to measure light sensitivity), and shutter speed, and much more. Click on and I'll walk you through all the freedome photography with a camera offers you, and how it may improve your art.
